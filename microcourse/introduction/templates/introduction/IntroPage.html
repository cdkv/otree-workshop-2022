Dear participant,


this experiment consists of several tasks. You will be rewarded for the completion of the experiment. In some tasks, you can earn extra money depending on your decisions. Some other tasks will not affect your own payment, but your choices will decide how money is distributed among other individuals. Note that these individuals will not be participants of this same experiment, but be recruited from the register of Finnish citizens for other tasks.

For each task, you will receive a separate introduction which explains the task, as well as the possible consequences. Any information provided to you will be accurate, and you will never be intentionally deceived by the instructions. Please read the instructions carefully.

During the tasks, payments are expressed in points. These points will be converted into EUR at the end of the experiment. Every 8 points during the experiment correspond to 0.01 EUR. This means that 800 points equal exactly 1 EUR.

You will earn 1600 points for completing the experiment, plus bonus payments that you earn during the different tasks. You will see a summary of your earnings at the end of the experiment.
